Edusavvy

Smart Education

Edusavvy served as a unique university community platform, bridging buyers and sellers within the campus. Through Edusavvy, students could access a wide range of educational resources and materials, enhancing their learning experience. The platform's community-driven approach fostered collaboration and knowledge sharing among students, creating a dynamic and engaging learning environment. Edusavvy aimed to simplify the process of buying and selling educational resources within the university, promoting a culture of academic excellence and innovation.

Agri Connect

Agriculture & Development

Agriconnect revolutionized the agricultural sector by directly connecting farmers with retailers, eliminating the need for intermediaries. This innovative platform empowered farmers to sell their produce at fair prices while providing retailers with a reliable and efficient supply chain. By bypassing traditional middlemen, Agriconnect ensured that farmers received fair compensation for their hard work while also offering retailers competitive prices. This direct connection streamlined the entire process, reducing costs for both farmers and retailers. Additionally, Agriconnect facilitated greater transparency in the agricultural market, enabling farmers and retailers to make informed decisions based on real-time data.
